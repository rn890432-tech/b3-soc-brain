## Code of Conduct

Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring environment for everyone. As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

• Using welcoming and inclusive language  
• Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
• Gracefully accepting constructive criticism  
• Focusing on what is best for the community  
• Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

• Harassment, trolling, or abusive comments  
• Public or private intimidation  
• Publishing others’ private information without consent  
• Discriminatory jokes or language  
• Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any unacceptable behavior.

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers at [your‑contact‑email@example.com]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

Consequences

• Warning – For minor infractions, a private warning may be issued.  
• Temporary Ban – For repeated or serious violations, contributors may be temporarily banned.  
• Permanent Ban – For severe violations, contributors may be permanently removed from the community.
